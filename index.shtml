THE MODERN FAMILY SHOW: WHO ARE WE?

Founded by Michael and Wes Johnson-Ellis, known as TwoDadsUK, Dads via UK Surrogacy. The Modern Family Show is an educational amily-building event taking place in the UK. A show like no other, designed to safely guide and support the LGBTQ+ community to understand their options for wanting children. Are you interested in; Surrogacy and IVF/IUI, Adoption, Fostering, Co-Parenting, Fertility Preservation, Solo Parenting, and Egg/Sperm Donation?

Find out more about us

WHY DID WE CREATE THE MODERN FAMILY SHOW?

Having witnessed discrimination firsthand when it comes to family-building as a gay couple, Michael and Wes wanted to create a safe space for the LGBTQ+ community to come and speak to experts, professionals and like-minded people.

WHY ATTEND THE SHOW...

The Modern Family Show provides you with the resources, the experts and give you the safe space you need to talk in private as part of our open surgery service. We can support the most exciting journey of your life ensuring you're better informed, whilst being treated with the respect you deserve.

We've designed The Modern Family Show to not only look and feel different, but to deliver an event that will help you take the next exciting step on a journey to building your family.
